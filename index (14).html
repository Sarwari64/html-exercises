Nostalgia:

Nostalgia refers to a sentimental longing or wistful affection for the past, typically for a period or place with happy personal associations. It often involves a yearning for a time when life seemed simpler, more enjoyable, or more meaningful. This feeling can be triggered by various stimuli, such as old photographs, music, scents, or even particular tastes that evoke memories of a specific time in one's life. Nostalgia can provide comfort and a sense of continuity but can also highlight the contrasts between the past and present.

Sentimentality:

Sentimentality refers to the quality or state of being excessively prone to feeling tenderness, sadness, or nostalgia. It often involves an emotional response that may be seen as exaggerated or overly dramatic. People who exhibit sentimentality might be moved to tears by things others find only mildly touching, or they might hold on to mementos and keepsakes with intense emotional attachment.
